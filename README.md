# 📘 Campus Drive Prediction System

## 📌 Overview
The **Campus Drive Prediction System** is a web-based application designed to evaluate and predict a student's placement eligibility during campus recruitment drives. It helps students understand their readiness for job opportunities by analyzing key academic and practical factors.

In today’s competitive environment, students often lack clarity about their placement chances. This system bridges that gap by providing an automated, data-driven assessment.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Predict student placement eligibility
- Provide actionable feedback for improvement
- Help students prepare better for campus drives
- Simulate real-world recruitment filtering systems

---

## ⚙️ Key Features
- 📊 **Eligibility Prediction** based on student data  
- 🎓 **Academic Analysis** using CGPA  
- 💻 **Skill Evaluation** based on technical competencies  
- 🛠️ **Project Assessment** considering practical experience  
- 📈 **Instant Feedback** with categorized results  
- 🧭 **Guidance Suggestions** for improvement  

---

## 🧠 Evaluation Parameters
The system evaluates students using three core metrics:

1. **CGPA (Academic Performance)**  
2. **Technical Skills**  
3. **Project Experience**  

---

## 🏷️ Output Categories
Based on the input, students are classified into eligibility tiers such as:
- ✅ Highly Eligible  
- ⚠️ Moderately Eligible  
- ❌ Needs Improvement  

---

## 💡 Benefits

### For Students:
